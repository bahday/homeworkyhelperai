Privacy Policy for Homeworky Helper AI

Last Updated: October 11, 2025

Introduction

Homeworky Helper AI ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you register for an account, we collect:

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Age (optional)
  • Date of birth (optional)
  • Parent information (if provided)

Usage Information

We collect:

  • Homework problems submitted for analysis
  • Images captured or uploaded through the camera feature
  • User progress and statistics (points, levels, achievements)
  • Session data and hints requested

Device Information

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Network connection status

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide AI-powered homework assistance
  • Analyze homework problems and provide hints
  • Track user progress and achievements
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Communicate with you about your account
  • Provide parental controls and monitoring features

Camera Permission

The app requests camera permission to:

  • Allow students to photograph homework problems
  • Enable image-based homework analysis
  • Capture homework for grading features

Important: Camera access is optional. You can still use the app by manually typing homework problems or selecting images from your gallery.

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption
  • Passwords are hashed and encrypted before storage
  • User data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard security measures
  • We do not sell or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini AI - For homework analysis and hint generation
  • Cloud hosting services - For data storage and API services

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

Children's Privacy

Homeworky Helper AI is designed for children aged 8-12 with parental consent. We:

  • Require parental email for child accounts
  • Provide parental controls and monitoring
  • Do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent
  • Comply with applicable children's privacy laws

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Export your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@homeworkyhelper.com

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request account deletion at any time through the app settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or via email.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@homeworkyhelper.com
  • Support: support@homeworkyhelper.com

Consent

By using Homeworky Helper AI, you consent to this Privacy Policy and our collection and use of information as described herein.
