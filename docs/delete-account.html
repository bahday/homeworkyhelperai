Delete Your Account

This page explains how to delete your Foghlaim AI account and what data will be removed.

Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. Once you delete your account, you will lose:

  • All your progress, points, and achievements
  • Your daily streak and level
  • Homework history and past sessions
  • Any saved preferences or settings

üì± How to Delete Your Account (In-App)

The easiest way to delete your account is directly within the Foghlaim AI app:

1 Open the App

Launch Foghlaim AI on your device

2 Go to Settings

Tap the "Settings" button on the main screen (gear icon)

3 Navigate to Account Settings

Scroll down to the "Account" section

4 Tap "Delete Account"

You'll find this option at the bottom of the Account section

5 Confirm Deletion

Read the warning message and confirm that you want to permanently delete your account

6 Account Deleted

Your account and all associated data will be immediately deleted from our servers

Response time: We will process your request within 48 hours and send you a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

üìù Data Retention Period

  • Immediate deletion: Most data is deleted immediately upon account deletion
  • Backup retention: Some data may remain in encrypted backups for up to 30 days for disaster recovery purposes, then permanently purged
  • Legal requirements: In rare cases, we may retain certain data if required by law (e.g., for ongoing investigations)
  • Anonymous analytics: Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics (no personal information) may be retained for product improvement

üîê What Data is NOT Deleted

The following data cannot be deleted because it doesn't identify you personally:

  • Anonymized usage statistics: Aggregate data like "500 users solved math problems today" (no personal information)
  • Server logs: Technical logs required for security and debugging (automatically expire after 90 days)

üîÑ What Happens After Deletion

  • You will be immediately logged out of all devices
  • Your username will become available for others to use
  • You cannot recover your account or data after deletion
  • You can create a new account anytime, but you'll start fresh with no previous data
  • If you had parent monitoring enabled, your parent will lose access to your dashboard

ü§î Alternatives to Deleting Your Account

Before you delete, consider these alternatives:

  • Take a break: You don't need to delete your account if you just want to stop using the app temporarily
  • Use Guest Mode: Log out and use the app anonymously (5 free questions per day)
  • Change your settings: Update privacy settings, disable notifications, or remove parent monitoring
  • Export your data: Contact us to request a copy of your data before deletion
